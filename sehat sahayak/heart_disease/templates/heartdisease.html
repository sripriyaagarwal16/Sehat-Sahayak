About THE Model

In predicting cardiovascular heart disease, we have used K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN), a simple yet effective machine learning algorithm. KNN works by finding the most similar data points (neighbors) in the training dataset for each new data point and making predictions based on their majority class. Our KNN model achieves an accuracy of 71%, indicating reliable predictions for about 70% of cases. However, for the remaining 30%, we strongly recommend consulting a doctor.Visit our doctor consultation page for expert advice and further assistance.
